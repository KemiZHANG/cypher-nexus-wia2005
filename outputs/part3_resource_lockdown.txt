Part 3 - The Resource Lockdown

Mission Problem
Select checkpoints that maximize operational impact while staying within energy, time, and token limits.

Algorithm Used
Dynamic Programming Knapsack

Rejected Algorithms
- Greedy selection is rejected because the highest immediate efficiency can block a better final combination.
- Brute force is rejected because checking every subset grows as O(2^n).
